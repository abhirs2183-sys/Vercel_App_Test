# yDatafix Design Guidelines

## Design Mandate
**CRITICAL**: Implement EXACTLY as shown in provided screenshots - pixel-perfect reproduction with no additions or modifications.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Cyan**: Light blue/cyan for accents, headings, buttons
- **Primary Navy**: Dark blue/navy blue for backgrounds and text
- **Accent Yellow**: For CTA buttons and highlights
- **Neutrals**: White and black for text and backgrounds
- **Dark Mode**: #0A1929 background, dark navy cards, white text, cyan accents
- **Light Mode**: Light gray/white background, light cards, dark text, cyan accents
